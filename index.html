Strays Poster

This emotionally driven poster aims to raise awareness about stray animals and promote adoption. With the tagline “They May Be Voiceless, But Their Hurt Speaks Volumes,” it combines raw imagery and heartfelt messaging to highlight the suffering of strays and encourage compassion. The muted, gritty texture reinforces the urgency of the issue, while the layout guides viewers to reflect and take action.

TICAP Cross Guild

This academic poster showcases TQC, a web-based electronic health record and mobile scheduling system developed for The Queen’s Birthing Home Clinic. The design presents a comprehensive view of the project, including its objectives, methodology, system interface, and evaluation results. The visual layout blends professionalism with clarity, effectively communicating the system’s goal to modernize clinic operations through secure data management and OCR technology integration.
